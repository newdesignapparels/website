About Us

New Design International Apparels is a trusted name in garment manufacturing, offering a wide range of T-shirts, uniforms, and sportswear for schools, corporates, events, and brands across India.

Our focus is on combining comfort, quality, and customization in every design. Whether it's cotton, polyester, or dry-fit fabric — our garments are crafted to provide the perfect blend of durability and style.

We offer complete custom branding solutions — embroidery, logo printing, and sublimation — all handled by our skilled in-house production team to ensure consistency and precision in every order.

With years of expertise, we’ve built strong relationships with clients who value our timely delivery, attention to detail, and innovative craftsmanship.

Company Milestones

Our journey of growth, innovation, and commitment — from a small unit in Tirupur to a recognized apparel manufacturer serving clients worldwide.

2018

New Design International Apparels was established in Thirupur, Tamil Nadu, with a mission to deliver quality apparel manufacturing solutions.

2019

Expanded operations to Valayangadu, Kerala with 3 production units, enabling bulk manufacturing with faster turnaround.

2020

Launched in-house DTF, sublimation, screen printing, and embroidery services, reducing outsourcing costs and improving quality control.

2021

Entered international markets, delivering customized apparel to GCC, European, and African countries.

2022

Strengthened sourcing partnerships with Ludhiana-based suppliers for premium, cost-effective fabrics.

2023

Expanded product line to include caps, chef coats, doctor/nurse uniforms, pants, shorts, and jerseys to serve diverse sectors.

2024

Partnered with major clients like Kairali TMT, Kenza TMT, Aqua Star, Euroguard, Image Mobiles and more, for promotional and branded merchandise.

2025

Expanded operations to Bangalore. Focused on innovation and customer experience with a vision to scale globally and introduce new fashion technologies.
