About Us

New Design International Apparels is a leading garment manufacturer based in India, specializing in T-shirts, uniforms, and custom sportswear for schools, corporates, and brands across the country.

Our mission is to deliver comfort, style, and durability through every stitch. We use premium cotton, polyester, and dry-fit fabrics to create apparel that not only looks good but also feels great to wear.

Whether you need custom logo printing, embroidery, or sublimation designs, our in-house team ensures top-notch quality and timely delivery. From bulk production to small-scale branding, we adapt to your needs with flexibility and precision.

With years of experience in the apparel industry, we’ve earned the trust of clients through our commitment to innovation, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.
