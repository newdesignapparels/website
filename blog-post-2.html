NDIA Expands Operations to Bangalore

Posted on October 31, 2025

We are thrilled to announce a major milestone in our journey! New Design International Apparels has officially expanded its operations with a new branch in Bangalore, Karnataka.

This expansion is a direct result of our commitment to growth, innovation, and, most importantly, serving our clients better. While our roots and primary manufacturing units are in Kerala and Tamil Nadu, this new presence in Bangalore—India's "Silicon Valley"—allows us to bring our high-quality custom apparel and services closer to the vibrant hub of corporate, tech, and startup clients in Karnataka.

Why Bangalore?

Bangalore is a city of innovation, speed, and immense corporate activity. We've seen a growing demand from tech companies, startups, and educational institutions in the region for premium-quality branded merchandise, professional uniforms, and event apparel.

Our new branch at Sarjapura, Azim Premji University Main Road, is strategically located to cater to this demand, offering:

  • Faster Turnaround: Local presence means quicker consultations, sample approvals, and delivery times for our Karnataka-based clients.
  • Dedicated Support: A local team ready to provide the same high-touch service and expertise that NDIA is known for.
  • Accessibility: An accessible touchpoint for new and existing clients to discuss their projects, view fabric samples, and see our quality firsthand.

Our Commitment Remains

While this is a new location, our core values remain unchanged. We will continue to leverage our complete in-house production capabilities—from design and fabric sourcing to DTF printing, sublimation, and embroidery—to ensure every order meets our high standards of quality and consistency.

We are incredibly grateful to our clients and partners who have supported our growth. This expansion is a significant step in our mission to become a globally trusted leader in custom apparel, and we look forward to building new relationships in Bangalore and beyond.
