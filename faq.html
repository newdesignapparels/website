Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We are flexible! We handle everything from single, personalized items for individuals to large-scale bulk orders for companies. Whether you need one T-shirt or 10,000, we can manage your request.

What customization options do you offer?

We offer a full range of in-house customization services, including:

  • DTF (Direct-to-Film) Printing: For high-detail, full-color designs on any fabric.
  • Sublimation Printing: For vibrant, permanent designs on polyester (great for jerseys).
  • Screen Printing: Cost-effective for bulk orders with simpler designs.
  • Embroidery: For a premium, 3D stitched look on polos, caps, and coats.
  • Labeling & Tags: Custom neck labels, wash care tags, and more for private label brands.

What types of fabrics can I choose from?

We source a variety of premium fabrics, including 100% Cotton, Polyester, Cotton-Poly Blends, Dry-Fit (for sportswear), and durable wovens like Twill and Canvas (for chef coats, pants, etc.). We can recommend the best fabric based on your needs for comfort, durability, and print style.

What is the typical turnaround time for an order?

Turnaround time depends on the order size and customization complexity. Because we have complete in-house production, we offer very competitive and fast delivery times. We even offer express production for urgent needs. Please contact us with your project details for a specific timeline.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we do. We have experience delivering customized apparel to clients in the GCC (UAE, Saudi Arabia, etc.), European, and African markets. We handle all export processes to ensure your order arrives securely and on time.

Can I get a quote for my project?

Absolutely! We offer free quotes. Please visit our Contact Page to send us a message, call us, or visit one of our branches to discuss your project. We're happy to help.
