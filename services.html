Printing & Customization

We specialize in turning your ideas into wearable reality. Whether it's a logo, name, or full artwork, we offer multiple customization methods to match your fabric, budget, and style.

DTF Printing (Direct-to-Film)

  • High-detail, full-color printing
  • Works on cotton, polyester, and blends
  • Ideal for logos, branding, and photo prints
  • Durable and stretch-friendly

Sublimation Printing

  • Vibrant, long-lasting prints
  • Best on white/light polyester fabrics
  • Used for sportswear, jerseys, and uniforms
  • Won't crack, peel, or fade easily

Screen Printing

  • Cost-effective for bulk orders
  • Sharp and solid colors
  • Ideal for simple designs and promotional wear
  • Long-lasting and washable

Embroidery

  • 3D stitch design for a premium look
  • Used for logos, initials, names
  • Great on caps, uniforms, chef coats, shirts
  • Adds texture, durability, and professionalism

Labeling & Tag Options

  • Private label stitching
  • Inside neck labels, wash care tags
  • Custom size/brand tags for retail-ready products

Fabric & Color Options

The right fabric defines how a garment feels, performs, and lasts. We offer a curated selection to bring your designs to life.

Cotton

Soft, breathable, and skin-friendly — perfect for all-day comfort in hot and humid climates. Cotton provides a natural feel and excellent air circulation, making it ideal for everyday wear, uniforms, and casual T-shirts. Available in multiple GSM weights and finishes.

Polyester

Lightweight, durable, and quick-drying — polyester is ideal for activewear. It retains shape even after multiple washes and resists shrinking or wrinkling. Commonly used in sublimation printing for vibrant, long-lasting colors.

Cotton-Poly Blend

A perfect balance of softness and strength. Blended fabrics offer the natural comfort of cotton with the resilience and wrinkle resistance of polyester. Ideal for workwear and uniforms where comfort and low maintenance matter.

Dry-Fit

Engineered for sports, Dry-Fit fabric wicks away sweat and allows for maximum flexibility. Its stretchable fibers ensure unrestricted movement, while maintaining a cool and lightweight feel — perfect for athletic jerseys and fitness wear.

Twill / Poplin / Canvas

Durable, structured, and built to last — these woven fabrics are commonly used for pants, chef coats, and industrial workwear. Twill offers a distinctive diagonal weave, Poplin brings a smooth texture, and Canvas ensures rugged durability.
